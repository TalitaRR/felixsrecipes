Cookies

Ingredients:

Bowl 1:

  • 1 cup bread flour
  • 2/3 all-purpose flour
  • sprinkle of salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder

Bowl 2:

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 tsp instant coffee
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg york
  • chocote chips as preferred

Method:

  • Preheat oven to 180C/ 350F
  • Mix ingridients in bowl 2
  • Slowly add ingridients from bowl 1 into bowl 2
  • Add chocolate chips and mix well
  • Roll golf ball sized balls
  • Place on a lined baking tray
  • Bake for 10 - 15 minutes
  • Enjoy!

Brownie

Ingredients:

Bowl 1:

  • 115g melted unsalted butter
  • 30g coco powder
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp espresso
  • 2 eggs

Bowl 2:

  • 100g all purpose flou
  • 20g bread flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking flour
  • pinch of salt

Method:

  • Preheat oven to 180C/ 350F
  • Mix ingridients in bowl 1
  • Add ingridients from bowl 2 into bowl 1
  • Gently fold together
  • Add the chocolate and fold in
  • Put in pan and bake for around 20 - 25 minutes
